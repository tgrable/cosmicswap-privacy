Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Cosmic Swap (also known as CosmicSwap), a match-three puzzle game for iOS.

Data We Collect

Cosmic Swap collects two types of data:

1. Crash Data

When the app crashes, we collect a crash report that may include: the type of crash, device model, iOS version, app version, and in-game event breadcrumbs (a log of recent actions leading up to the crash). This data is sent to Sentry (sentry.io) and is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

Crash data is not linked to your identity and is not used for advertising or tracking.

2. Per-Install Device Identifier

Cosmic Swap generates a random identifier when you install the app. This identifier is stored locally on your device and resets if you reinstall the app. It is used to count how many distinct devices are affected by a given crash, which helps us prioritize fixes.

This identifier is not an advertising identifier (it is not your IDFA). It is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking or advertising.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Third-party analytics
  • Account data of any kind

Tracking and Advertising

Cosmic Swap does not track you across apps or websites. Your data is never used for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Crash data is processed by Sentry, which has its own privacy policy. No other third-party data processors are used.

Data Retention

Crash reports are retained by Sentry for 90 days. The per-install identifier exists only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Your Choices

You may opt out of crash reporting by uninstalling the app. There are no in-app settings to disable crash collection at this time.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
