Support

Cosmic Swap - Match-Three Puzzle Game

Report a Bug or Issue

If you've found a bug or are having trouble with Cosmic Swap, please open an issue on our GitHub repository:

github.com/tgrable/CosmicSwap/issues

When reporting an issue, it helps to include:

  • What happened and what you expected to happen
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Steps to reproduce the problem

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Cosmic Swap collect my personal data?

No. Cosmic Swap does not collect your name, email, location, or any personal information. The only data collected is anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does Cosmic Swap contain ads?

No. Cosmic Swap does not display ads or use any advertising services.

Does Cosmic Swap require an internet connection?

No. The game works fully offline. An internet connection is only used to send crash reports if the app encounters an error.
